# Los Angeles One-day Travel Notes

- Griffith Observatory is best in early morning or sunset; parking can be crowded in noon.
- The Getty Center has timed entry suggestions and usually lower crowd on weekday mornings.
- Santa Monica is windy in late afternoon; bring a light jacket.
- Consider grouping attractions by area to reduce cross-city traffic time.
- Reserve at least 20 minutes transfer buffer between two major locations.
