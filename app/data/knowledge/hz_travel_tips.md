# Hangzhou One-day Travel Notes

- West Lake is always worth a visit, no matter what time or whether; parking can be crowded in noon.
- The tea museums have timed entry suggestions and usually lower crowd on weekday mornings.
- Hangzhou in March is always rainy; bring an umbrella.
- Consider grouping attractions by area to reduce cross-city traffic time.
- Reserve at least 20 minutes transfer buffer between two major locations.
